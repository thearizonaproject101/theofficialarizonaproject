About Us

The Arizona Project is dedicated to ensuring that every student has access to meals during the school day. Through third party funding, we hope that no student has to go hungry.

Our Program

We deliver free lunches to students who need them the most. Our goal is to reach as many students as possible. Via donations and volunteers, we will be able to expand our reach.
